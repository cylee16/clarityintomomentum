About me

Hi, I’m Cylee — and I’ve been where you are.

I know what it feels like to be capable, motivated, and completely stuck all at the same time. That tension is exactly what led me to this work — and it's why I'm so passionate about helping other women find their way through it.

My story

From overwhelmed to on purpose.

There came a point in my life when I realized that doing more wasn’t the answer. I was busy, I was trying hard, and I still felt dim — disconnected from myself and unsure of what I actually wanted. I was tired. I was stressed. And somewhere along the way, I stopped feeling like myself anymore. (Not to mention perimenopause hitting like a freight train!)

The woman who used to be excited and outgoing just wanted to close off and crawl into bed. I kept waiting for the season to pass on its own — for things to magically click back into place. But they didn’t. And eventually I had to admit that what I really needed wasn’t another to-do list or a stricter routine. I needed to reconnect with who I actually was underneath all of it. Sound familiar?

That season was the beginning of a real shift. It pushed me to get serious about understanding why we get stuck, how real change actually happens, and what it looks like to build a life with intention instead of just momentum.

I pursued my certifications through Health Coach Institute — earning both my Life & Nutrition and Advanced Health Coach credentials — and I'm currently working toward board certification through NBHWC. But more than credentials, what I bring to this work is lived experience: the kind that makes me genuinely curious about your story, not just your goals.

I work with women who are motivated but stuck — busy moms, women navigating midlife, women who feel like they've lost the thread of who they are. I see you. And I believe you already have more than enough to move forward. You just need a clearer path.
