“I was stuck. I’ve worked with personal trainers before. I know what workouts to do, what foods I should be eating, the importance of sleep, hydration — all the things. But after a major life transition and move, I just couldn’t seem to get myself back on track.

That’s when Cylee became a coach, and I asked if I could be her first client. It was exactly what I needed. Through weekly sessions, motivational messages, and consistent check-ins, she helped me work through the mental and emotional barriers that were keeping me from making progress. Instead of focusing only on workouts and nutrition, she helped me understand what was really holding me back and gave me the support and accountability I needed to move forward.

What made it even more valuable was knowing I was working with someone who truly understood. She’s faced many of the same challenges with hormones, food, body changes, and life transitions, so her guidance came from both knowledge and personal experience.

If you’re feeling stuck and struggling to reconnect with your goals, she’s the kind of coach who will meet you where you are, help you overcome what’s holding you back, and cheer you on every step of the way.”
